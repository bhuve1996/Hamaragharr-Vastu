## üì¶ FORMAT & QUALITY

### File Formats
- **Logos/Icons**: PNG (transparent) or SVG
- **Product Photos**: JPG (80-90% quality) or WebP
- **Banners**: JPG (optimized) or WebP
- **Favicon**: PNG (square, transparent)

### File Size Limits
- Product Images: Under 500KB
- Banner Images: Under 300KB
- Logo/Icons: Under 100KB
- Favicon: Under 50KB

### Color Space
- **sRGB** for all web images (not CMYK)

### Optimization
- Compress before upload (TinyPNG, ImageOptim, Squoosh)
- Shopify auto-generates multiple sizes
- Theme supports lazy loading

## ‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **Shopify Auto-Generation**: Shopify creates multiple sizes automatically - provide highest quality/largest size needed
2. **Aspect Ratios**: Maintaining correct ratios is crucial - images will be cropped if ratios don't match
3. **Team Photos**: Only needed if you have a team section on your About page. Team photos are **portrait** (taller than wide), not square!
4. **Mobile Optimization**: Provide separate mobile versions for banners/hero sections when possible
5. **Product Images**: Provide 4-6 images per product (multiple angles)
6. **Alt Text**: Prepare descriptive alt text for SEO and accessibility
